Temple Highlight: Lisbon, Portugal Temple

The Lisbon, Portugal temple is the first in the country, and it was dedicated earlier this year on September 15, 2019. It was announced by President Monson in the October 2010 General Conference. This is the fourteenth temple in Europe, and prior to this temple, the 45,000 members in the area had to travel 390 miles to Madrid, Spain for the nearest temple.
