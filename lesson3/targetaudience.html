Target Audience

Persona 1:

Male, 56 years old, father and grandfather. Has lived in Preston his whole life. Potato farmer who is very mindful of the weather as it affects the livelihood of his farm operation, which his whole family depends on. Uses his home desktop computer to check the weather each night and morning, and wants comprehensive, detailed information.

Persona 2:

Female, 34 years old, mother of three young children. Lives in Soda Springs, and keeps track of the weather to plan her day and know how to dress the kids for school. Generally uses her mobile device to browse the web, and casually checks on the weather when it’s on her mind.

Scenarios

  • The weather is looking really bad outside this morning… Let’s see if it’s cold enough for school to be cancelled.
  • I need to see the temperature trends for the last several years in the Preston area so I can determine when we should plant this spring.
  • I wonder what Fish Haven looks like in March.
  • I’m heading to Soda Springs sometime today and I want to make sure to get there before the storm hits. Let’s look at the radar and find out what time I should leave.
  • Is it warm enough to go boating on Bear Lake today?
