Site Name: Sunny Days Ahead

I decided to name my website “Sunny Days Ahead” because I think creates a great theme for my website. I noticed that the towns we’ll be including in the site are towns in Idaho, and I know that Idaho can always use some more sunny days, so I decided it would be a fun theme for my site! I’ve found the domain name sunnydaysahead.net is available for only $12 a year.

Site Purpose

The purpose of my site is to provide weather information and related resources to residents of the towns I’ll be focusing on. I also want my site to be bright, cheerful, and positive, going in line with its name, “Sunny Days Ahead”. Some of the content objectives for each town will include current weather information, historic weather trends, images, graphs, and links to external resources.
